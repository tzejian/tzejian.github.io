Project DBS LMBL

I was responsible for website maintenance, developing new modules tailored to client specifications, encompassing both Chinese and English versions.

The website was efficiently managed without relying on a CMS. Instead, we leveraged JSON to store data such as article titles, image numbers, tags, and estimated reading time. This streamlined data reuse across the website, saving time and minimizing the risk of errors compared to manual content population.
