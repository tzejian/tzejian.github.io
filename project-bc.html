Project Binary Coffee

For this project, I had the privilege of coding a single-page static HTML site designed by an agency. It was a challenging and rewarding experience to bring the agency's vision to life through coding, and I am proud to have delivered a site that met their expectations.

I worked closely with the agency to ensure that their design was accurately reflected in the final product, and the end result was a seamless user experience that showcased the client's brand and message.
