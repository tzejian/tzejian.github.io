Video Edit: Diving

I had the opportunity to film and edit a comprehensive video of an open water diving course trip, from start to finish. Using Premiere Pro, I worked on the footage to create a final product that was engaging and told the story of the trip effectively. It was a challenging project for me, as it was my first time working with such a large amount of footage, but I was able to successfully navigate through it all and produce a high-quality end result.

This was a challenging project, as I had to condense a lot of information and footage into a short, impactful video. However, with my attention to detail and ability to convey a compelling story, I was able to deliver a video that effectively promoted the diving expo to potential attendees.
