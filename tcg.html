I designed a mockup of a trading card game inspired by the neumorphism design trend, which combines elements of skeuomorphism and flat design. This design trend is known for its soft, extruded plastic look that mimics physical objects, creating a sense of depth through shadows and highlights.

Neumorphism is popular for its minimalist aesthetic and focus on simplicity and usability.
