Project Data Science Guild

For this project, I had the opportunity to work with SharePoint and use its list feature as a pseudo back-end to save data.

SharePoint is a powerful platform that allows businesses to collaborate and manage their data efficiently, and I was excited to explore its capabilities in web development.

Using SharePoint lists as a back-end allowed me to create a seamless user experience, where data can be saved and retrieved easily without the need for complex databases. It also provided a centralized location for data management, ensuring that information is consistent and up-to-date across the website.

I believe that using the right tools and technologies is essential to creating successful websites, and SharePoint is one such tool that has proven its worth in data management and collaboration. I am always eager to learn and explore new technologies to create better and more efficient websites for my clients.
