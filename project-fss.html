Project FSS Diving School

My first foray into web development was a freelance project for a diving school, and unfortunately, it did not meet the client's expectations.

While this project was a learning experience, it was also a failure that taught me the importance of effective communication with clients and setting realistic expectations.

Despite the setback, I was determined to improve my skills and create better websites in the future. This project also gave me the opportunity to experiment with parallax effects, a technique that creates an illusion of depth on the website.

While the outcome may not have been successful, it sparked my passion for web development and inspired me to push my creative limits to create visually stunning and engaging websites for my clients.
