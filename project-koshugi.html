Branding Design experiment with ChatGPT

Using ChatGPT to roleplay as a client to experiment with a new branding.

With this I can leverage the power of ChatGPT to simulate conversations and interactions between a client and a branding professional. This allows me to explore different scenarios and test various branding strategies without involving actual clients or making real-world commitments.

By assuming the role of a client, I can immerse myself in the mindset of my target audience and simulate their needs, preferences, and expectations. Through this roleplay exercise, I can engage in dynamic conversations with ChatGPT, exploring different branding ideas, discussing design concepts, evaluating marketing approaches, and even receiving feedback on my proposed branding strategies.

Design Brief

Objective:
Create a brand logo for koshugi that evokes a sense of nostalgia and fun, and appeals to collectors and enthusiasts of trading card games, with a particular focus on Pokemon TCG.

Target Audience:
Collectors and enthusiasts of all ages who value high-quality products and services, and are passionate about trading card games, especially Pokemon TCG.

Brand Personality:
Playful, bold, memorable, retro, and fun.

Design Guidelines:

  • Incorporate bold typography with a retro feel that stands out and is easy to read.

  • Use bright colors to catch the eye and create a playful, fun vibe.

  • Consider incorporating vintage graphics or imagery that relates to trading card games or Pokemon TCG in particular.

  • The logo should be versatile enough to be used across various platforms and media, including website, social media, print, and packaging.

Deliverables:

  • A vector-based logo in a high-resolution format (e.g. AI, EPS, or SVG).

  • A style guide outlining the color palette, typography, and any other design elements used in the logo.
